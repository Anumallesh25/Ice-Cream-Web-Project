Ice cream may be served in dishes, eaten with a spoon, or licked from edible wafer ice cream cones held by the hands as finger food.
Ice cream may be served with other desserts such as cake or pie or used as an ingredient in cold dishes like ice cream floats,sundaes, milkshakes,
and ice cream cakes or in baked items such as Baked Alaska.Ice cream is a colloidal emulsion made with water, ice, milk fat, milk protein, sugar and air. Water and fat have the highest proportions by weight creating an emulsion that has dispersed phase as fat globules.
